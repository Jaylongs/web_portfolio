Adobo Recipe

Adobo is my favorite dish made with meat, vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and spices. It's savory, tangy, and absolutely delicious!

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken or pork, cut into serving pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the meat, soy sauce, and garlic. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the marinated meat and cook until browned.
  3. Add the onions and cook until softened.
  4. Pour in the vinegar and let it boil without stirring.
  5. Add the bay leaves, peppercorns, and water. Bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce the heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the meat is tender.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice.
